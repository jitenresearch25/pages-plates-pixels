About PPP

Pages • Plates • Pixels

A small creative home for stories, cooking, mathematics, photography, and everyday learning.

What is PPP?

Pages are for stories, poems, notes, and reflections. Plates are for cooking experiments and homemade food. Pixels are for everyday photos and small creative posts.

This website started as a simple GitHub Pages project. The goal is not perfection on day one. The goal is to keep learning, posting, improving, and slowly building a warm home for creative work.

Here you will find small LaTeX beauties, Hindi and English writings, travel memories from Budapest and Vienna, and honest homemade food posts — not always perfect, but always made with heart.

Learning and creating, one small post at a time.
