Today’s LaTeX Beauty: derivative of e^x

A function so special, its rate of change is itself.

Today’s tiny LaTeX beauty:

\[\frac{d}{dx}e^x=e^x\]

In calculus, the derivative tells us how something changes. But e^x is special — its rate of change is itself.

Sometimes mathematics quietly says something beautiful: some things grow, change, and still remain true to themselves.
