A Flower in the Mud

A tiny glimpse from a larger story — just enough to open the door.

Every great journey begins with a small whisper.

The boy found a flower growing in the mud. Everyone passed by without looking at it. Some even stepped on the soft earth around it. But the boy stopped.

He did not know why, but the flower looked familiar — as if it had been waiting for him.

He touched the ground gently. For a moment, he thought he heard the earth whisper: “You came back.”

This is only a small glimpse from a larger world I am slowly building.
