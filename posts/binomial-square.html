Today’s LaTeX Beauty: (a+b)^2

A simple formula with a small life lesson hidden in the middle term.

Today’s tiny LaTeX beauty:

\[(a+b)^2=a^2+2ab+b^2\]

Most of us learned this formula in school, but it still has a small beauty hidden inside it.

When two quantities come together, the result is not just their individual parts. There is also a connection between them — the middle term 2ab. Sometimes mathematics quietly teaches life too.
