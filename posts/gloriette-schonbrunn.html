Gloriette, Schönbrunn Palace Gardens

A quiet reflection of Vienna’s royal beauty — calm sky, garden water, and the Gloriette standing gracefully above Schönbrunn.

Behind Schönbrunn Palace, the Gloriette stands gracefully above the gardens, mirrored softly in the water below. The cloudy sky made the scene even more peaceful — not bright, not dramatic, just beautifully calm.

Location: Gloriette, Schönbrunn Palace Gardens, Vienna, Austria.
