Today’s LaTeX Beauty: sin x over x

Small changes can reveal big truths.

Sometimes the smallest expressions carry the deepest meanings.

Today’s tiny LaTeX beauty:

\[\lim_{x\to 0}\frac{\sin x}{x}=1\]

This simple-looking limit is one of the foundations of calculus. It helps us understand derivatives, trigonometric functions, and how mathematics behaves near zero.

Mathematics often teaches us one thing: small changes can reveal big truths.
